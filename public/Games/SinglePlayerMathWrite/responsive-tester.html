üß™ How to Test

Desktop Browser Testing

  • Open the application in your browser
  • Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  • Click the device toolbar icon or press Ctrl+Shift+M
  • Select different devices from the dropdown
  • Toggle between portrait and landscape orientations
  • Resize the browser window to test all breakpoints

Real Device Testing

  • Open the application on your phone or tablet
  • Rotate your device to test both orientations
  • Check that margins are even on all sides
  • Verify no scrollbars appear
  • Ensure all content is visible without scrolling

What to Look For

  • Even margins on top, bottom, left, and right
  • No horizontal scrollbar at any screen size
  • No vertical scrollbar on main screens
  • All buttons are easily tappable (48√ó48px minimum)
  • Text is always readable (not too small)
  • Canvas maintains proper aspect ratio
  • Content is always centered and balanced
